### Remove comments at the beginning of a file

```regex
<!--
 \* @Description: (\S)+
 \* @Author: (\S)+
 \* @Date: (\S)* (\S)+
 \* @LastEditors: (\S)+
 \* @LastEditTime: (\S)+ (\S)+
 -->\n
```
